# Emotion Pattern Analysis

## Research Motivation
I aim to model emotional and mental states as structured data
to support future AI-based personalized mental health systems.

## Research Question
- Can emotional states be quantified?
- Are there relationships between emotion and sleep?
- Emotional states have relation with individual depression?

## Method
- Self-reported emotional data
- Statistical and correlation analysis

## Outcome
Basic patterns between sleep, stress, mood, mental disease were observed.

## Next Step
Extend to text-based emotion inference.
